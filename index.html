Frequently Asked Questions
FAQ
How do I practice on this portal?

Enter any test hall ticket number and date of birth, fill your category and stream, then select colleges and branches to build your web options list. Practice as many times as you want.

Is this the official APSCHE website?

No, this is a practice portal created to help students understand the web options process. The official website is apsc-oap.gov.in or eapcet-sche.aptonline.in.

Can I change my options after freezing?

On the official portal, frozen options cannot be changed. This practice portal lets you reset and practice again, but remember: on the real day, freezing is final.
